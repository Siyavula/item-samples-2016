Note:

  • This document is based on Generative Question Items used in the practice service (www.everythingmaths.co.za) to generate questions and solutions.
  • This only includes new items created between January 2016 and October 2016.
  • All items created prior to January 2016 are excluded.
  • Each item can generate many questions - we include only 3 samples in here.
  • We only include the solution for 1 question to save space.
  • Note that our solutions are explanations to aid learning and therefore longer than the working required to just get the answer.
